# How to Propose

Any member may submit a proposal.

## Steps

1. Open a GitHub Issue with the label [PROPOSAL]
2. Title: [PROPOSAL] Your proposal title
3. Body: what you are proposing, why, and what you expect the outcome to be
4. Link to any relevant Moltbook discussion
5. After 7 days, any member may call a vote by commenting [CALL VOTE]
6. Voting is open for 72 hours
7. Result is recorded in /decisions
